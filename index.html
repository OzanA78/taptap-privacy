Privacy Policy for TapTap

Last updated: April 24, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how TapTap ("we", "our", or "the app") handles information when you use our application.

Information We Collect

Data Stored Locally on Your Device

TapTap collects and stores the following data exclusively on your device:

  • Game preferences and settings
  • High scores

This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to any server.

Advertising (Google AdMob)

We use Google AdMob to display banner advertisements. AdMob may collect and use data to serve personalized ads, including device identifiers and usage data. For more information, see Google's Privacy Policy.

Remote Configuration (Firebase Remote Config)

We use Firebase Remote Config to manage app settings remotely, such as minimum required app version. No personal data is collected through this service.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with third parties, except as described above through Firebase and AdMob services (Google LLC).

Data Retention

Locally stored data remains on your device until you uninstall the app or clear app data.

Children's Privacy

TapTap is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above.
